How it works
Three steps to a tighter edit
1

Load your video

Drag and drop any .mp4, .mov, .mkv, or .avi file — up to 120 minutes. Supports standard, VR, and 360° formats.

2

Review auto-detected scenes

Scene boundaries are found automatically. Each segment is scored by motion and audio energy, color-coded green to red.

3

Export your edit

Set a target duration, hit Auto-select, or pick segments manually. Export a clean, re-encoded video in one click.
